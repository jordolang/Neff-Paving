## Getting Started

### What You'll Need

Before scheduling your appointment, please have the following information ready:

- **Project Address**: Full address where the work will be performed
- **Contact Information**: Your name, email, and phone number
- **Project Details**: Type of service needed (driveway, walkway, parking lot, etc.)
- **Preferred Date Range**: When you'd like the consultation or work to begin
- **Project Size**: Approximate square footage or dimensions

### Service Types Available

- **Residential Services**: Driveways, walkways, patios, small repairs
- **Commercial Services**: Parking lots, sidewalks, loading docks, large-scale projects
- **Emergency Services**: Urgent repairs, safety-related issues
- **Maintenance Services**: Sealcoating, crack repair, line striping

## Scheduling Your Appointment

### Step 1: Access the Scheduling System

1. Visit our website at [www.neffpaving.com]
2. Click on **"Schedule Consultation"** or **"Book Appointment"**
3. You'll be directed to our Calendly scheduling interface

### Step 2: Select Service Type

Choose the appropriate service category:

- **Residential Consultation** (60 minutes) - For home projects
- **Commercial Consultation** (90 minutes) - For business projects  
- **Emergency Service Call** (30 minutes) - For urgent issues
- **Maintenance Consultation** (45 minutes) - For existing pavement care

### Step 3: Choose Date and Time

1. **View Available Slots**: The calendar shows all available appointment times
2. **Select Your Preferred Time**: Click on an available slot that works for your schedule
3. **Time Zone**: Appointments are shown in your local time zone

### Step 4: Provide Project Information

Fill out the required information:

**Contact Details**:
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number

**Project Information**:
- Project Address
- Type of Project (driveway, walkway, parking lot, etc.)
- Estimated Square Footage
- Preferred Start Date
- Special Requirements or Notes

**Additional Questions** (depending on service type):
- Urgency Level (for emergency services)
- Business Name (for commercial projects)
- Contact Person on Site
- Deadline Requirements

### Step 5: Confirm Your Appointment

1. **Review Details**: Double-check all information for accuracy
2. **Confirm Booking**: Click **"Schedule Event"** to finalize your appointment
3. **Confirmation**: You'll receive an immediate confirmation on screen

### Step 6: Confirmation Communications

After booking, you'll receive:

- **Instant Confirmation**: On-screen confirmation with appointment details
- **Email Confirmation**: Detailed email with appointment information and our contact details
- **Calendar Invitation**: Add the appointment directly to your calendar
- **Reminder Notifications**: Automatic reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before your appointment

## Managing Your Appointment

### Rescheduling Your Appointment

If you need to change your appointment time:

1. **Use the Link in Your Email**: Click the "Reschedule" link in your confirmation email
2. **Select New Time**: Choose from available time slots
3. **Confirm Changes**: Your updated appointment will be confirmed automatically
4. **New Confirmation**: You'll receive updated confirmation details

### Canceling Your Appointment

To cancel your appointment:

1. **Use the Cancellation Link**: Click "Cancel" in your confirmation email
2. **Confirm Cancellation**: Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation
3. **Cancellation Notice**: You'll receive confirmation of the cancellation

### Appointment Reminders

You'll automatically receive:
- **Email Reminder**: 24 hours before your appointment
- **SMS Reminder**: 1 hour before your appointment (if phone number provided)
- **Final Confirmation**: Day-of reminder with our team's contact information

## Preparing for Your Appointment

### Before Our Team Arrives

- **Clear Access**: Ensure the project area is accessible
- **Mark Utilities**: Contact 811 (Dig Safe) if excavation might be needed
- **Gather Information**: Have any previous estimates, permits, or property surveys available
- **Decision Makers**: Ensure decision-makers are available during the consultation

### During the Consultation

Our team will:
- **Assess the Project**: Evaluate the scope and requirements
- **Provide Recommendations**: Suggest the best approach for your needs
- **Discuss Timeline**: Review project schedule and timing
- **Present Options**: Explain different service levels and pricing

### What to Expect

- **Punctuality**: Our team arrives within the scheduled time window
- **Professional Assessment**: Thorough evaluation of your project needs
- **Detailed Estimate**: Written proposal with clear pricing and timeline
- **No Pressure**: Take time to review our proposal - no immediate decision required

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Scheduling Questions

**Q: How far in advance can I schedule?**
A: You can schedule up to 6 months in advance. For urgent needs, we often have same-day or next-day availability for consultations.

**Q: What if I don't see any available times?**
A: Try selecting a different service type or date range. For urgent needs, call us directly at (555) 123-4567.

**Q: Can I schedule outside business hours?**
A: Our standard hours are 7 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday. Emergency services may be available outside these hours.

**Q: Is there a fee for the consultation?**
A: Consultations are free for most residential and commercial projects. Emergency service calls may have a nominal fee.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The scheduling page won't load**
A: Try refreshing your browser or clearing your cache. If problems persist, call us at (555) 123-4567.

**Q: I'm not receiving confirmation emails**
A: Check your spam folder first. Ensure the email address is entered correctly. Contact us if you still don't receive confirmation.

**Q: Can I schedule on my mobile phone?**
A: Yes! Our scheduling system works on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile.

### Service Questions

**Q: What happens if it rains on my scheduled day?**
A: Weather-dependent work will be automatically rescheduled. You'll be notified as soon as possible about any weather delays.

**Q: Do you provide estimates for all project types?**
A: We provide estimates for most paving projects. Specialized or very large projects may require additional assessment.

**Q: How long does a consultation take?**
A: Residential consultations typically take 30-60 minutes. Commercial projects may require 60-90 minutes.
