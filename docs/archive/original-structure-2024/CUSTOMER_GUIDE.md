# Customer Guide

## Overview

This guide provides comprehensive instructions for customers using the Neff Paving scheduling system. Our online platform makes it easy to schedule appointments, manage your bookings, and get the paving services you need.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

1. **Website**: Visit [neffpaving.com](https://neffpaving.com)
2. **Direct Scheduling**: Use our online scheduling tool
3. **Mobile Access**: The system is fully mobile-responsive
4. **Phone Support**: Call (555) 123-4567 for assistance

### Service Area Coverage

Our paving services are available in the following areas:
- Metro area within 50-mile radius
- Residential and commercial properties
- Emergency services available 24/7
- Free estimates for all projects

## Scheduling Your Appointment

### Online Scheduling Process

1. **Visit Our Website**: Go to neffpaving.com
2. **Select Service Type**: Choose from our available services
3. **Pick Date and Time**: Select your preferred appointment slot
4. **Provide Details**: Enter project information and contact details
5. **Confirm Booking**: Review and confirm your appointment

### Required Information

When scheduling, please provide:
- **Contact Information**: Name, phone, email
- **Property Address**: Complete address for the job site
- **Project Details**: Type of paving work needed
- **Preferred Timing**: Date and time preferences
- **Access Requirements**: Any special access needs

### Service Types Available

- **Asphalt Paving**: Driveways, parking lots, roadways
- **Concrete Work**: Sidewalks, patios, foundations
- **Repair Services**: Crack filling, resurfacing, patching
- **Maintenance**: Sealcoating, line striping, cleaning
- **Emergency Services**: 24/7 urgent repairs

## Managing Your Appointment

### Viewing Your Booking

- **Confirmation Email**: You'll receive immediate confirmation
- **Appointment Details**: Date, time, service type, and technician
- **Preparation Instructions**: What to do before we arrive
- **Contact Information**: Direct line to your assigned team

### Modifying Your Appointment

To reschedule or modify your appointment:
1. **Call Our Office**: (555) 123-4567 during business hours
2. **Email Request**: Send changes to scheduling@neffpaving.com
3. **Online Portal**: Use the appointment management link in your confirmation
4. **24-Hour Notice**: Please provide at least 24 hours notice for changes

### Cancellation Policy

- **24-Hour Notice**: Free cancellation with 24+ hours notice
- **Same-Day Cancellation**: May incur a service fee
- **Emergency Cancellation**: Contact us immediately for urgent changes
- **Refund Policy**: Full refund for advance cancellations

## Preparation for Your Appointment

### Before We Arrive

**Property Preparation**:
- Clear the work area of vehicles and obstacles
- Ensure access to the job site is available
- Remove any decorative items or furniture
- Notify neighbors if necessary

**Utilities and Safety**:
- Mark any underground utilities (call 811 if needed)
- Ensure pets are secured
- Designate a safe area for equipment
- Provide access to water if required

### During the Service

**What to Expect**:
- Professional, uniformed technicians
- All necessary equipment and materials
- Safety protocols and protective measures
- Regular updates on progress

**Your Role**:
- Be available for questions and decisions
- Provide access to electrical outlets if needed
- Keep children and pets away from work area
- Communicate any concerns immediately

## Payment and Billing

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How far in advance should I schedule?**
A: We recommend scheduling 1-2 weeks in advance for optimal availability.

**Q: Do you provide free estimates?**
A: Yes, all estimates are free with no obligation.

**Q: What happens if it rains?**
A: Weather-dependent work will be rescheduled automatically.

**Q: Do you offer emergency services?**
A: Yes, 24/7 emergency services are available at (555) 123-HELP.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How long does paving take?**
A: Timeline depends on project size - from 1 day for small driveways to several weeks for large commercial projects.

**Q: How long before I can use the paved surface?**
A: Typically 24-48 hours for foot traffic, 3-7 days for vehicle traffic.

**Q: Do you handle permits?**
A: Yes, we handle all necessary permits and inspections.

**Q: What warranty do you provide?**
A: All work comes with a comprehensive warranty - details in your contract.

### Billing Questions

**Q: When is payment due?**
A: Payment is due upon completion unless special arrangements are made.

**Q: Do you offer payment plans?**
A: Yes, financing options are available for qualified customers.

**Q: Can I get a detailed breakdown?**
A: Yes, all invoices include detailed breakdowns of materials and labor.
